I'm David Romero, a computer science teacher.

This is a personal blog. And, as such, it will capture whatever I feel like writing at certain moments: reflections, tutorials, experiences, or even collaborations with other people if the opportunity arises. Therefore, I must warn you that everything expressed here reflects only an opinion at a specific moment, which may change the very next minute after its publication. It's also possible that the texts contain a large percentage of irony. I like to play with ambiguity. My deepest apologies if this aspect drives you crazy, if you can't handle cognitive dissonance or if you confuse it with inconsistency.

Thank you for reading. I hope to hear from you soon as well.
